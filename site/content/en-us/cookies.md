We use cookies to help us understand how you use our website, allowing us to provide you with a better browsing experience. Cookies also help us identify which parts of the website are the most popular, determine areas that may require improvement, and ensure that the content we provide is relevant to you.

### How do we use cookies?

We use cookies to store items in your shopping cart while you browse our website, remember your search preferences when you return to the site, and help us improve our services. We also use these technologies to analyze traffic patterns on our website and identify any areas of concern. By continuing to browse this website, you agree that we may place these types of cookies on your device.

### What types of cookies do we use?

**Session Cookies** – These are temporary cookies that remain in your browser until they expire or are deleted through a user action, such as closing the browser window or clicking a link. They do not store personal information.

**Persistent Cookies** – These cookies remain on your device for a specified period (or until manually deleted). They allow us to recognize your device whenever you visit our website so that we can tailor content to your interests.

They also help us remember choices you make while using our website, such as language preferences or geographic location. These cookies are necessary for certain features of our website to function properly.

**Third-Party Cookies** – These cookies are set by third parties and enable them to recognize your device when you visit their websites, allowing them to provide content tailored to your interests.

### How long are cookies stored?

The retention period for cookies depends on how long they are required to provide a service. Cookies may remain stored on your device for as long as necessary to deliver a service or until you choose to delete them.

### How can you manage cookies?

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish.

For example, you can modify your browser settings to block cookies, preventing your device from accepting new cookies, or you can delete all cookies that have already been stored on your device.

You may also configure your browser to notify you whenever a cookie is stored on your computer or mobile device.

The following links provide information on how to manage and delete cookies in the most common web browsers:

- Google Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
- Mozilla Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer
- Microsoft Edge: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge
- Safari (Mac): https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac
- Opera: https://help.opera.com/en/latest/web-preferences/#cookies
