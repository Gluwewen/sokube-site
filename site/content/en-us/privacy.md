At SoKube, we take the protection of your personal data seriously. We want you to be confident that we will use your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

The purpose of this document is to provide you with a description of the personal data we collect from you, how we use it, and the circumstances under which we may share it with other entities. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will handle it.

### Who is covered by this Data Privacy Policy?

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the website, including, but not limited to, registered and non-registered users.

### What personal data do we collect?

We collect certain basic information about you when you register to use our services, such as your name, email address, telephone number(s), and postal address. When you contact us by email, we also collect any information contained within those emails (for example, the content of the message).

We may also collect information from other sources (such as public records), including IP address, location data (GPS), information about your device or computer, including its operating system and browser type, as well as cookies placed on your computer by third parties who advertise on our behalf or provide analytics tools or other services related to our business.

### How do we use this personal data?

We use this information to respond to comments submitted through our website, process information requests made through our website, organize contests or promotional campaigns, and send information about our products and services.

We also use personal information to personalize the content you see on our website, improve our services, and better understand how visitors use our website.

### Why do we process your personal data?

We process your personal information for various purposes, including:

- Responding to comments or questions you submit;
- Providing a service or product that you have requested;
- Recording and managing sales of our products and services;
- Sending information about our products and services (including special offers);
- Communicating with you and sharing news and updates;
- Allowing you to follow us on social media;
- Enabling you to receive our newsletter, which provides updates about our offers, services, and products based on your consent.

### How long do we retain your personal data?

We will retain your personal data for as long as required by law. This means that we will keep it at least until the end of the calendar year following your last transaction with us, or until you request that we delete it.

After that, unless other legal obligations require us to retain your data, we will remove it from our systems.

### What rights do you have regarding your personal data?

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data and to ask us to correct, update, or delete it if it is inaccurate. You also have the right to withdraw your consent for us to process your data at any time. If you would like further information or wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer by email at [contact@sokube.io](mailto:contact@sokube.io). You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data. You also have the right to object to the processing of your data for legitimate reasons.

### Do we use cookies?

We use cookies to ensure that we provide you with the best possible experience on our website. If you continue to use the website without changing your settings, we will assume that you are happy to receive all cookies from SoKube. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, please disable or delete them from your browser settings.

### Who has access to your data and how do we protect it?

Our data is only accessible to authorized members of our team. We maintain an internal information security policy that outlines the measures we take to protect your data. We take appropriate steps to ensure that your data is not exposed, lost, or stolen. In the event of a security breach, we will notify you promptly and inform you of any actions you can take to protect yourself from potential harm.

### Can your data be transferred outside the European Union?

We may transfer your data outside the European Union, but only where we have a lawful basis for doing so. If we transfer your data outside the European Union, it will be to a country that provides an adequate level of protection for personal data.

### Who can you contact for more information about the processing of your personal data?

You may contact us at any time for further information regarding the processing of your personal data.
