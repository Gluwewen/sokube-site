## Investigate faster, resolve smarter
Incidents on modern platforms rarely come from a single component. A deployment, an infrastructure change, a configuration drift, or an external dependency can produce symptoms far from their real cause.

Engineers often spend more time gathering information than solving the problem.

NeuroKube acts as an operational investigation assistant. It collects, organizes, and correlates information across the whole platform, through a simple conversation.

### What slows investigation down
* Operational data is scattered across multiple tools
* Critical context is hard to reconstruct
* Root cause analysis relies heavily on individual expertise
* Escalations create bottlenecks
* Resolution time grows with platform complexity

### Built for platform teams
NeuroKube is designed for organizations running cloud-native platforms, where an incident often involves multiple systems, teams, and technologies at once.

By reducing the effort of collecting and correlating operational context, NeuroKube lets engineers focus on what matters: solving the problem and improving platform reliability.

AI assistance stays transparent, explainable, and fully controlled by your engineering teams.

## The result
By supporting engineers throughout the investigation, NeuroKube helps organizations:
* Reduce mean time to resolution, cutting MTTR by 3x to 10x on common incidents
* Improve investigation consistency
* Reduce dependency on the same experts
* Preserve the operational knowledge produced by each investigation

AI does not replace engineering judgment. It gives engineers the context to exercise it faster.
