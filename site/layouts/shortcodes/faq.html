{{/* FAQ Shortcode Renders an accordion-style FAQ, grouped by category with a sticky side nav when at least one question has a category (falls back to a single flat list otherwise). Site-wide shortcode: one visual identity for the whole site, defaults live in [params.faq.style] (hugo.toml). Toggle the whole shortcode on/off via [params.sections].faq. category nav text, active state (default: params.faq.style.menuActiveText) Minimal call: {{}} title: "Frequently Asked Questions" questions: - question: "..." answer: "..." {{}} Full call with categories and a one-off color override: {{}} title: "Most frequently asked questions" description: "Find answers to frequently asked questions." questions: -
